Graceful Degradation vs. Progressive Enhancement

Graceful Degradation

to create and serve up the best experience possible, and then account for each possible degradation and ensure that despite any shortcomings, the site would remain functional


In terms of mobile web design, this meant that a full, standard website would scale back and gradually remove content and features as the viewport became smaller and the system simpler (no Flash support, etc.).

Progressive Enhancement

You put your best foot forward on the mobile platform, providing the users with minimal screen real estate, processing power and third party plugins an amazing experience that both looks great and functions perfectly. As the need arises, the site can gradually be “enhanced” and even completely rethought for larger platforms with fewer constraints.
